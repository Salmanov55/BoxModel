Kuba

Cuban culture is a mixture of ethnic culture brought from different regions of the world. From the 16th century, slaves brought to work on Cuban plantations later mingled with white colonists and indigenous people to form the modern Cubans as a nation.

Dubai

The Gulf War in early 1991 had a negative financial effect on the city, as depositors withdrew their money and traders withdrew their trade, but subsequently, the city recovered in a changing political climate and thrived.
